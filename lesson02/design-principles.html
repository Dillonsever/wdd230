Visual Hierarchy

Website: Gym Shark

Description: This site demonstrates effective visual hierarchy through the use of size, contrast, and positioning of products. They place certain things in different postitons in order to catch your attention.

White Space and Clean Design

Website: Nike

Description: This site uses white space to create a clean and organized webiste that is user friendly. It uses the spaces to create emotion and a very pleasing feeling.

Rule of Thirds

Website: Adidas

Description: The design follows the rule of thirds, placing important elements along the grid lines. This helps with making the site look professional and accurate.
